AI Advisory & System Design

We work with organisations building AI systems that interact directly with people.

Our focus is on behaviour, interaction, and system design, particularly in environments where uncertainty, safety, and human context matter.

We often work at the intersection of product, research, and system design, where traditional roles or teams do not yet fully exist.

  • Behavioural dynamics in human-AI interaction
  • Adaptive system behaviour and response design
  • Responsible AI and safety by design
  • Emotional and cognitive signal interpretation
  • Early-stage AI system architecture
